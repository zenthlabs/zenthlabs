Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Offline & Local Processing

At Zenth Labs, we believe your data should never leave your sight. Most of our tools are designed to be fully functional without an internet connection.

100% Offline Capability: All core utilities, including Criptix and SmartKit, process your information locally on your device. We do not use external servers to handle your sensitive data.

Zero Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Whether you are encrypting text or calculating financial data, the process remains private.

  • No account registration is required to use our tools.
  • We do not track your usage behavior or search history.
  • Your inputs are temporary and are cleared as soon as the session ends.

Advertising Transparency

To keep our services free for everyone, we may display advertisements. Our ad policy is built on privacy:

  • Non-Personalized: We do not use tracking cookies to serve targeted ads based on your personal identity.
  • Random Rotation: The advertisements shown are random and not based on your private data or browsing history.
  • No user data is ever sold or shared with third-party advertisers.

Security Standards

By utilizing local-first architecture, we eliminate the risk of server-side data breaches. Your device acts as the sole vault for any information processed within Zenth Labs applications.

Contact & Feedback

If you have questions regarding this policy or any privacy concerns, feel free to reach out to our team.

Email: support@zenthlabs.com
